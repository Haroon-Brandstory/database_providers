Cookie Policy & Cookie Settings

What Are Cookies

Cookies are tiny pieces of information that are saved on your device when you navigate to a site. They assist the site in identifying you, recollecting your likes and dislikes, and making your experience better. Database Providers also rely on cookies to securely run our website, assess the flow of visitors, and to deliver a good and customized browsing experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are fundamental for providing the basic functionality, security, and accessibility of the website. They are crucial for the proper operation of the site and, thus, cannot be turned off.

Analytics Cookies

These cookies give us insights into the website usage by the visitors which, in turn, allows us to gradually enhance the site in terms of performance and user-friendliness.

Functional Cookies

These cookies keep the information about user's preferences, for example, language, location, or region, and thus, provide users with a more personalized experience.

Marketing Cookies

These cookies are meant for serving the relevant ads and re-marketing purposes. The use of such cookies is permitted only when you give your consent through our cookie banner or settings panel.

Consent & Management

The first time you access our website, a cookie notification will show up with the option to either allow all cookies or categorize and manage your preferences. Also, you have the right to alter or revoke your consent whenever you want; just click on "Cookie Settings" located in the footer of our website.

Third-Party Cookies

We may allow certain trusted third-party partners (such as analytics or advertising providers) to set cookies on our website. Each third party maintains its own privacy and cookie policies. Where required by law, we will obtain your consent before enabling any marketing or tracking cookies.

How to Control or Remove Cookies

You can manage, block, or delete cookies at any time through your web browser settings. For detailed instructions, refer to your browser's help pages. Please note that disabling some cookies—especially essential ones—may limit certain website features or reduce functionality.

Cookie Retention

  • Session cookies: Expire when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: Remain on your device for a period defined by the cookie provider.
  • We do not use cookies to collect personally identifiable information without your explicit consent.
